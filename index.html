About GDSC

Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) is a Google Developers program for university students to learn mobile and web development skills. The club will be open to any student, ranging from novice developers who are just starting, to advanced developers who want to further their skills. The club is intended as a space for students to try out new ideas and collaborate to solve mobile and web development problems.

Empowering Students Through Technology

The GDSC-SVCE is dedicated to leveraging technology for the betterment of the community. We provide a platform for students to explore the latest in tech and develop solutions that make a difference.

THE MEMBERS

Members will embark on a curated technical learning journey, fostering a dynamic learning community, while also actively contributing to the planning and execution of events.

MEMBER OBJECTIVES

  • Customized Learning Path specific to domain: Working on various hands-on projects that enhance the learning approach.
  • Provide Guidance and Resources: Encourage teams to seek help and feedback from peers and mentors.
  • Event Planning and Management: Event management, Event planning, Promotion, etc., which enhances management skills.

ASSOCIATES

Associates will foster their own expertise in the technical fields while guiding members in gaining domain knowledge. They will also play a key role in curation, collaboration, and construction with our events, hackathons, and ventures like such.

ASSOCIATE OBJECTIVES

  • Curators of Growth: Guide members, assign tasks, and track progress.
  • Collaborators:
    • Facilitate as a bridge between technicalities and operations.
    • Facilitate effective communication and collaboration at team GDSC.
    • Ensure that all our Club ventures are aligned with the micro and macro-level goals we set.
  • Connectors: Bridge the gap between leads and members, ensuring clear communication.

GDSC First Meetup

On September 2, 2024, the Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) and its members from various subdomains, including web development, app development, problem solving, cloud development, marketing, and machine learning gathered for its first introductory meet up. The event fostered a collaborative environment where members discussed upcoming event planning and shared insights into their respective fields. The gathering included a general discussion with leads, allowing everyone to introduce themselves and connect more deeply, enhancing our sense of community within the club.

A highlight of the event was the opportunity for each member to pitch an idea in under 20 seconds. This fast-paced session encouraged creativity and quick thinking, allowing everyone to showcase their unique perspectives and project ideas. The enthusiastic participation and valuable feedback generated a wealth of ideas, marking the event as a successful initiative by GDSC SVCE and strengthening our collaborative spirit.
